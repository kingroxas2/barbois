WORKOUT PROGRAMS

Stretch It Out!

There is a big chance that you are stiff or already have some pains before, during,
or after your calisthenics workout. Thus, stretching needs to be correctly prior to workouts
in order to allow your muscles loosen up and become more resistant to the impact they
be put to, lowering the risk of injury. Remember to always stretch before a workout!

Our Circuit Programs

Circuit training is the best step in improving strength, endurance, muscle mass, and even weight loss.
Just prep yourself up with hard work and dedication, you will surely get the results you desire. These are the 4 workout routines we have prepared for you;

Beginner Circuit Workout

Do all the exercises in the order shown, resting 30 seconds between exercises and 3 minutes afterward. Repeat for 3 rounds.

1. Pull-ups (10x)
2. Chin-ups (10x)
3. Dips (20x)
4. Jump squats (25x)
5. Push-ups (20x)
6. Crunches (50x)
7. Burpees (10x)
8. Jump rope (30 seconds)

Intermediate Circuit Workout

Rest 5 seconds between exercises and 8 minutes at the end of one round. Repeat for 2 rounds.

1. Muscle-ups (5x)
2. Push-ups (50x)
3. Jump squats (25x)
4. Burpees (15x)
5. Pull-ups (15x)
6. Leg flutters (60 seconds)
7. Pull-ups (10x)
8. Non-stop sprint (30 seconds)

Advanced Circuit Workout

Perform each exercise for 30 seconds straight. Do not rest between exercise. Complete 1 round.

1. Hold a handstand (30 seconds)
2. Jump squats
3. Wall push-ups
4. Kick up push-ups
5. Squat position move side-to-side
6. X's and O's core workout
7. Dips
8. Jumping lunges
9. Hops
10. Pull-ups

Ultimate Circuit Workout

Rest 30 seconds between exercises but no rest between rounds. Do 3 rounds.

1. Back lever hold (10 seconds)
2. Flag hold (7 seconds)
3. Front lever hold (5 seconds)
4. Bent arm planche hold (15 seconds)
5. Handstand hold (30 seconds)
6. Very slow muscle-ups (3x)
7. Slow dips (10x)
8. Pull-up hold position (30 seconds)
